Chicken Fried Rice

This is some delicious fried rice! Loaded with protein, carbs, and minerals to keep you on the go all day every day. Super simple and delicious. INGREDIENTS

  • 500g Chicken
  • 1 Cup Basmati Rice
  • 1/2 tbsp Ginger
  • 6 Cloves of Garlic
  • 1tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 Cup Mixed Veg
  • 1 Cup Water
  • 1tbsp Sesame Oil
  • Seasonings for chicken
  • 3tbsp soy sauce
  1. Make Rice and Chicken at the same time
  2. Mix Rice, Veg, Soy Sauce, and sesame oil
